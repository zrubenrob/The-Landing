The Landing Gym

The Landing is a brand-new, large-scale, high-end fitness and wellness facility opening in Niceville, Florida.

It is going to be a large one-story building with a lot of different areas: a spacious gym floor with premium equipment, a recovery and stretching area, men’s and women’s locker rooms with saunas, a Montessori-style childcare center, indoor and outdoor pickleball courts, and an outdoor running track that wraps around the whole property. The goal is to make this a premium, family-friendly destination where people can work out while their kids are watched, all in one place.
